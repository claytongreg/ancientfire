About the Dojo

A way place for
those seeking return.

Gather Inspire Thrive

Ancient Fire is a Dojo — a ‘way place’ — dedicated to the practice of self progression.

It is sanctuary. A house of service, geared towards seeking the point of origin: our self.

01 — Breath

Breathwork
as meditation.

We offer breathwork as a meditation — held for groups, or privately, one on one.

A practice for arriving home to the body. For hearing what the quiet has been trying to say.

02 — Body

Martial arts

A variety of specialized traditions, studied with reverence and held with rigor.

  • Jeet Kune Do
  • Kali
  • Eskrima
  • Silat
  • Kickboxing
  • Muay Thai
  • Savate
03 — Place

Salt Spring
Island.

Located on the beautiful west coast of Salt Spring Island, we offer an oasis of opportunity — to learn, to grow, to practice, and to deepen.
