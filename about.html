About the Dojo

A way place for
those seeking return.

Gather Inspire Thrive

Ancient Fire is a Dojo — a ‘way place’ — dedicated to the practice of self progression.

It is sanctuary. A house of service, geared towards seeking the point of origin: our self.

The Flame

The ancient fire is the sacred flame within. The place we are returning to.

There is a deep remembrance happening within humanity as a whole — an awakening to who we truly are. But awakening asks something of us. It asks that we clean it up. That we learn to live with integrity and humility.

To dive deep and clear the ancestral traumas and wounds carried in our lineage — the ones passed down, and the ones we carry now. This is the work. This is the path. One that can be chosen as an effective tool for transformation.
